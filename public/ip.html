IP Block Solutions

Problem #1

Error Message: "Sorry, this IP address has been blocked from playing the FREE PLAY game. You may continue to use the rest of the website as normal."

Cause:

Email address is not verified.

Possibles Solutions:

  • Search on your email box for an email (noreplay@freebitco.in) sent to verify your email address.
  • Sign in on freebitco.in on your main browser (the same when click on every hyperlink).
  • Click on the link in that email.
  • Done.
If you do not find the email about verification, I suggest you change the email on Profile Tab inside Freebitcoin. You will probably receive a new email on the new email address to verify it.

Problem #2

Error Message: "Sorry, this IP address has been blocked. If you are using a proxy, VPN or anonymization service, please turn it off before claiming free bitcoins. If you are not using any of the above,please let us know using the contact form in the FAQ page.

Cause:

VPN is enabled or an Anonymization App is installed on your mobile.

Possibles Solutions:

  • First: Go to youtube and search for DISABLE VPN ON ANDROID and watch it.
  • Second: Uninstall all of your VPN like APP such as Hola VPN, Turbo VPN, VPN Master, SuperVPN, or whatever.
  • Done.
Check you IP/Location here. That must show your city/location, if that is not your city/location, you are using VPN.

Problem #3

Error Message: "Time is running even though you did not play? It's probably because another user is playing from the same IP as you."

Cause:

Someone's else is playing freebitcoin using the same IP as you.

Possibles Solutions:

  • Click on the blue hyperlink below the error message to verify you mobile number and keep playing.
  • Done.
You only are able to verify your mobile number using that hyperlink that appear with this error message. There no other way to verify your number.
